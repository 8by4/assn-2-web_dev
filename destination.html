Travelogue: Nagoya, Japan

Overview:

Overall, Nagoya has been my favorite place I have ever traveled ever. The city itself is absolutely gorgeous with amazing attractions. Outside of this, the suburban areas nearby are just as entertaining and can often even be more enjoyable. With areas such as the Ghibli park or NUFS nearby, it's easy to see why I loved this location so much!

Must-see Spots:

  1. Studio Ghilbi Park
  2. Osu Shopping District
  3. Sakae Underground Mall
  4. Nagoya Castle
  5. Legoland Japan

Visitor Tips:

  • Be quiet and polite on the trains.
  • Always say thank you and goodbye.
  • Do not tip! It's considered rude.
  • Walk on the opposite side of the sidewalk.
